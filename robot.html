Game plan.

Our game plan for Rover Ruckus enables us to score 100+ points if executed properly, ~75 if not.

However, our latch mechanism is not completed as of competition date - points expected have been reduced drastically, and any compensation depends on the drivers and luck.

Autonomous

  • Maybe(drop off lander) - 30 points - Not Yet Implemented
  • Pick up the gold block - 25 points - Not Yet Implemented
  • Park at crater(or partially) - 10 points

    • Approximately 10 points so far

    Teleop

  • Score beginning minerals - 20 seconds, 2 silver, 10 points - Implemented, depends on drivers
  • Score gold cubes and silver balls (3 runs of either, 2 per run, 30 points) - Implemented, depends on drivers

    • Approximately 50 points so far



    Endgame

  • If we have any cubes left from teleop, score those - Implemented, depends on drivers
  • Latch back onto lander(assuming if hooked on from start) - Not Yet Implemented
  • Park in the crater completely if we do not latch - 25 points - Implemented, depends on drivers

    • Approximately 75 points in total
